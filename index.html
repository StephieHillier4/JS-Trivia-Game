How to Play

Welcome to my Seinfeld Trivia Game! To get started, simply click on the "Start Seinfeld Trivia" link to arrive at the question section. There are 40 questions in total plus a BONUS SECTION. Each question will have multiple choice style answers that are worth 5 points each and with each wrong question, a player loses 5 points. Once you reach 40 points, YOU WIN THE GAME! If you get more than 4 wrong questions, YOU LOSE! A player's score will show at the bottom of the screen so it's easy to keep track of their progress.

Bonus Section

The bonus section allows players to double the points (i.e. 10 points instead of 5 points) especially if it's the difference between winning and losing or if they just want to test their true knowledge. For example, if a player already has 3 wrong answers and 30 points, the bonus question will give the player the extra 10 points to win the game! There are 4 bonus questions, one on each main character, and the player can choose which main character they want to test their knowledge on. HOWEVER, there are NO multiple choice answers and should a player choose a bonus question and get it wrong they automatically LOSE the game!
